About The Website

This site was inspired by all of those personal websites that people used to have in the 90's and early 2000's (note that I was not around back then). Only partway through development did I discover the wonders of neocitiesand all of the cool sites on there. Anyways, I think it's a shame that those kinds of personal websites are becoming kind of rare nowadays, so I wanted to do my part and make a website that I really like, instead of one that was just serviceable.

It also serves as a bit of a dev profile, which also helps to fill out some more space on these pages.

The source code for this website can be found here. In general, I don't really mind others using portions of my work, just do not claim entire projects of mine as yours.

The fonts used are:

  • Secondary: VCR OSD MONO
